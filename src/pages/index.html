Our Cause

In 2013, Marylou Stover founded a children’s daycare house under the name of “El Castillo de los Sueños de Jennifer”. Marylou was a Canadian citizen who witnessed how much Los Algodones needed a place for children to stay while their moms were working. Soon after its founding, what use to be a children's daycare became a refuge to those kids who didn't have a roof of their own. With hard work and will, “El Castillo de los Sueños de Jennifer” sheltered 19 children giving them a safe place to feel secure and protected.

On May 2015, Marylou Stover passed away leaving behind what she had built. Since then, Mike and Janice Fischer have taken care of the house, now re-named “Take My Hand”, with the help of Delia Parra. They have put a big effort in following the path that Marylou Stover began, but the resources are fewer every day as the needs of the children grow.

Los Algodones Fundraising Event 2016 is the first edition of what we expect to be the beginning of a big change for Los Algodones. We want to honor the mission that Marylou Stover started because we believe in it. With the help of everyone reached by our message, we can make a difference in these children's lives. We want to ask for your help to spread hope to all the people of this town. “Take My Hand” is not only our first mission, it's the declaration of better times coming for Los Algodones.

We invite you to be part of our event and enjoy all our artistic offer. Help us make a change. Help us build something beautiful. The big heart of Los Algodones is built by you, so attend its call. Because everything is possible if we are united. Because we are Better Together.

If you want to know more about the house and its activities, you can visit the blog where Janice Fischer has been writing about Take My Hand.
